Our Programs

Welcome to our programs designed to transform the future of aquaculture. We offer initiatives such as the Youth in Sustainable Aquaculture and the Aquaculture Livelihood Improvement Program, which help small and medium-sized fish farmers thrive. Our mission also focuses on empowering women and youth to engage in fish production, ensuring a profitable and sustainable future for all. Join us in making a difference in the aquaculture sector!

Yisa support young people especially the women in creating dignified and fullfilling work opportunities in aquaculture sector.
