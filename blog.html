Frywell: Empowering Women Through Fish Business

Frywell is a newly launched program dedicated to empowering women aged between 18 to 35 years. The program offers hands-on training on fish preparation and healthy cooking practices.

Upon completion, each participant receives startup support that includes fish stock worth Ksh 12,000, a cooler box, stove, apron, umbrella, cooking spoon, and a frying pan, among other essentials.

This initiative not only promotes entrepreneurship but also enhances food hygiene and economic independence for young women in the community.

Our goal drives us to do all in our power to power fish farming for small and medium-sized fish farmers by developing creative and modern fish farming methods using cutting-edge technology
