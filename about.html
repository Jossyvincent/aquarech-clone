Powering Growth in Aquaculture

Who are We :Aquarech is Kenya's first fish farming platform that enable fish farmers, fish feed manufacturers, and fish Traders to trade and build trusting long-lasting partnerships.

Our Vision : Aquarech is on track towards becoming Africa's premier fish farming platform for small and medium-sized fish farmers, feed makers, and fish buyers wishing to buy and sell feeds, fish, and practice sustainable fish farming.

If you haven't heard of us yet, we guarantee you won't be able to forget us once you do. Our team is comprised of only the best employees in the industry, all of whom work tirelessly to achieve the objectives they have set for themselves.
